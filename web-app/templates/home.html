Predestination. It'll blow your mind.

Predestination (2014) is based upon a 1959 short story titled ‘All You Zombies‘ by sci-fi writer Robert A.Heinlein, and is a stylish time-travel movie exploring the paradoxical nature of time travel. With its intricate chronology of events, this cerebral sci-fi thriller navigates through multiple twists of fate as the story’s tragic key character is gradually revealed to be self-created entity trapped within a closed loop in time.
